# The 1975 Hippie Trail Journey - AI Story Video

## Overview

This project transforms Bob Campana's 1975 world journey into an AI-generated cinematic short video.

The story follows his travels through Indonesia, India, Nepal, Lebanon, and Europe, highlighting cultural experiences, personal growth, and entrepreneurial inspiration.

## Tools Used

* ChatGPT
* Google Flow
* VN Editor

## Workflow

1. Story Interpretation
2. Scene Design
3. AI Image Generation
4. AI Video Creation
5. Voiceover Development
6. Subtitle Integration
7. Final Editing

## Skills Demonstrated

* Generative AI
* AI Storytelling
* Prompt Engineering
* Video Production
* Creative Editing

## Deliverables

* AI Images
* AI Video Clips
* Workflow Documentation
* Final Video Output

## Outcome

Successfully converted a real-life travel and adventure story into a cinematic AI-generated video experience.
